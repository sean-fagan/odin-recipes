Lasagna

Each forkful offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, with the hearty sauce complementing the tender noodles and melted cheese. We hope you enjoy our step by step guide for our favorite lasagna recipe!

Ingredients

  • Meat
    • Sweet Italian sausage
    • Lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices and seasonings
    • Fresh parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black pepper
    • Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
